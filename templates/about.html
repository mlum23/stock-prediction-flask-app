How are predictions made?

Back to main page

Predictions are being made with a simple linear regression model. Every time a submission is made, the backend is creating a model with a 80-20 train-test split on the Yahoo Finance stock data going back up to 5 years. That is, 80% of the dataset (chosen randomly) is used for training the model, while the other 20% is used for validating the model.


Some advantages of linear regression is that it is simple to implement and is computationally efficient. Some disadvantages include being prone to outliers and underfitting.
